# Ideas for the Future

## 🃏 Reusable Slide Deck Creator
Turn this presentation setup into a generalised skill/template that can be used to create other slide decks — define slides in a `slides.ts` config, drop in your own images and ASCII art, and get a beautiful custom presentation site.

## 📱 Fix Mobile Layout
The current presentation is designed for desktop/projector. Mobile layout is broken — responsive styles needed for the slide components, text panels, and ASCII art sizing.

## 📖 Vertical Scrolling Visual Essay
Once the talk is done, transform the content into a long-form scrollable visual essay. Each section becomes a chapter with the ASCII art as ambient background, and the speaker notes expanded into proper prose. A really distinctive piece of design writing.
